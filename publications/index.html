Humanity Award

On the 19th Health Asia International exhibition in Karachi, We were in collaboration with the Liaquat University Hospital Hyderabad/Jamshoro seminar on “Let’s Talk about the Period” care of women with bleeding disorders. It was a very successful and informative seminar, credit goes. Dr. Arshi Naz, her team, and Hemophilia Welfare Society Karachi HWSK's team. Hematologists and Gynecologist speakers provided valuable and useful insights to all participants, especially the Bleeding Disorders community..

19TH HEALTH ASIA CONFERENCE

On the 19th Health Asia International exhibition in Karachi, We were in collaboration with the Liaquat University Hospital Hyderabad/Jamshoro seminar on “Let’s Talk about the Period” care of women with bleeding disorders. It was a very successful and informative seminar, credit goes. Dr. Arshi Naz, her team, and Hemophilia Welfare Society Karachi HWSK's team. Hematologists and Gynecologist speakers provided valuable and useful insights to all participants, especially the Bleeding Disorders community.

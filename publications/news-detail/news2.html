HUMANITY AWARD

The Hemophilia Welfare Society Karachi has been honored with the Humanity Award.

HUMANITY AWARDS (Champions for Change) Honouring Heroes Who Helped Affectess of Recent Rains & Floods in Pakistan.

Recent disasters and human tragedies have occurred due to floods and heavy rains in Pakistan . In this situation, like other national and international NGOs, our leadership and team were also engaged in playing their role in providing relief and other services and treatment facilities to the victims, especially for persons with bleeding disorders including hemophilia.

The credit for organizing this unique event goes to Metro 1 News TV Channel and especially to host program Metro Matters Mr. Akhtar Shaheen Rind Page & his team who invited around 100 of organizations from across the country to the Sindh Boy Scouts Association Auditorium Karachi and presented shields from the hands of the Sindh Governor Mr. Kamran Khan Tessori & from Mr. Haji Muhammad Hanif Tayyab in recognition of their services.

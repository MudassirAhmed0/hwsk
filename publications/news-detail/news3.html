Let’s Talk about the Period care of women bleeding disorders

On the 19th Health Asia International exhibition in Karachi, We were in collaboration with the Liaquat University Hospital Hyderabad/Jamshoro seminar on “Let’s Talk about the Period” care of women with bleeding disorders. It was a very successful and informative seminar, credit goes. Dr. Arshi Naz, her team, and Hemophilia Welfare Society Karachi HWSK's team.

Hematologists and Gynecologist speakers provided valuable and useful insights to all participants, especially the Bleeding Disorders community.

Raheel Ahmed talked about the statistics of women with bleeding disorders in Pakistan, their challenges, ground realities, advanced treatment, and protocols, He also said that there are very competent hematologists in public hospitals but the hematology departments are non-functional in managing hemophilia and other bleeding disorders due to the non-availability of basic coagulation testing facilities and CFCs, and for this purpose, HWSK in the initial process with Sindh Government, and it is hoped that soon under a public-private partnership the hematology departments can be made efficient and effective.

He also said that there is a need to work together with more robust and close coordination between HWSK and gynecologists to care for women with bleeding disorders.

He also talked about the women's group of Hemophilia Societies, how they are working on treatment, awareness, and empowerment, and how Hemophilia Foundation - Pakistan and other international organizations are providing their support to them. Dr. Munira Borhany provided awareness to women with bleeding disorders in very good and easy words, she said that due to improper diagnosis, women do not get timely and better treatment, so proper diagnosis should be ensured at the beginning. She also gave advice and information to parents and girls about monthly check-ups and making a menstrual chart.
